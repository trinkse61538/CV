Mautic - Automation Email Marketing

Systematizing data management and automating end-to-end customer journeys

Centralized Contact Management

This is the hub for storing and managing all customer identity information. The system allows me to track detailed data from Emails and Phone Numbers to Points (Lead Scoring) and Last Active history. Centralized management enables high-level personalization for all subsequent marketing campaigns.

Multi-path Automation Flow

Automated workflows allow me to nurture leads in real-time. Based on logical conditions (Email Opened, Link Clicked, Wait 3 days...), the system automatically categorizes and delivers relevant content tailored to the customer's specific psychological stage, increasing conversion rates without manual intervention.

Dynamic Audience Segmentation

I utilize Segments to micro-target the database based on behavior and intent. Dynamic grouping ensures that every message sent is highly relevant to the recipient, preventing spam flags and maintaining the highest possible Open Rates.

Email Engagement Analysis

A deep-dive measurement tool for campaign effectiveness. I analyze metrics such as Click Heatmaps, Click-through Rates (CTR), and read time to evaluate content quality. These insights form the basis for A/B testing, allowing for continuous optimization of email performance.
